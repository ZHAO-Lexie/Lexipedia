Basic Information

Gotta say my skill tree’s kinda niche but focused – wanna guess? PS, painting, photography, editing. All artsy stuff – shocker, right?

Been sketching since elementary school, even considered art school. Plot twist: chose academics instead. Still kept the skill alive though – upgraded from paper to iPad Procreate glow-up (bless that app).

In uni, joined the publicity squad – got thrown into poster design, yearbooks, even costume making. Crash-course taught myself PS, later scored photo-editing side hustles. Shoutout to my mom for gifting me that Canon 80D – became my portrait magic wand.

Post-grad journalism era? Became video editing trash panda – cut everything from news clips to TikTok fluff to promo reels. Tried all the fancy software. Turns out? CapCut’s the holy grail.

Now back in school studying humanities… but HKBU’s sneaking coding/AI into my curriculum? Wild. Currently nerding out over building websites – hence this digital meet-cute. Life’s weird.
